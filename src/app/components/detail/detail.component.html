Tikona (Vitandgad/वितंडगड)

Distance
  • From Pune - 60 Km
  • From Mumbai - 133 Km
History

One of the prime forts of Maratha empire, Tikona Fort, was seized by Mughals for a short period. Shivaji Maharaj took it under his regime in the 1670s from Mughals. Till Sambhaji ruled this fort; it was a significant influence to Marathas. Signs of a broken temple can be seen till date in the Fort that provides glimpses of its history

Pawna Lake (पवना तलाव)

Distance
  • From Pune - 63 Km
  • From Mumbai - 103 Km
History

Situated 11 km away from Lonavala, Pawna Lake is an artificial lake which is surrounded by immense beauty. Formed by the dam built across the Pawna river, the lake is a perfect spot for picnics. The road leading to the lake is a quiet and serene drive with refreshing greenery and craggy hills passing you by.
